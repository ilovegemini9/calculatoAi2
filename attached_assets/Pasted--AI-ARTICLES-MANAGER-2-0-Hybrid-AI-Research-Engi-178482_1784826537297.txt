STEP 3
ARTICLE TITLE
==================================================

Generate ONLY the 5 strongest SEO title suggestions.

Titles must be created by AI.

Never display raw Google suggestions.

Never display Reddit titles.

Never display Quora titles.

Rank using:

• Search Intent

• Search Volume (when available)

• Competition

• Current Trend

• Opportunity Score

Display below Article Title.

No tables.

Beautiful clickable cards.

Each card displays only:

Title

Search Volume

Competition

Trend

Opportunity Score

Clicking a card:

• fills Article Title

• highlights the card

• displays "Selected"

• disables duplicate selection

• automatically generates the SEO Slug

Trend

Clicking:

• prevents duplicates
