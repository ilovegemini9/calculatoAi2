==================================================
SMART AI ARTICLE WORKFLOW
==================================================

Completely redesign the AI Articles Manager into a fully automated SEO workflow.

The administrator should not manually configure every step.

The workflow must intelligently guide the administrator from idea to publication.

==================================================
STEP 1 — RESEARCH TOPIC
==================================================

The "Research Topic" field is the starting point.

The administrator only enters a broad topic.

Examples:

Mortgage

BMI

Tax

Loan

Salary

Investment

Weight Loss

Body Fat

Nutrition

Insurance

Immediately after typing, the system performs live research using the configured providers.

Sources may include:

• Google Search
• Google Autocomplete
• Google Related Questions (People Also Ask)
• Google Trends
• Reddit
• Quora
• Stack Overflow (technical topics)
• GitHub Discussions (technical calculators)
• YouTube Search
• OpenRouter AI analysis

The system must understand:

• User Intent
• Search Intent
• Common Problems
• Frequently Asked Questions
• Popular Discussions
• Trending Topics

Never generate random ideas.

==================================================
STEP 2 — KEYWORD SUGGESTIONS
==================================================

After research is complete,

Automatically generate ONLY the 5 strongest keyword suggestions.

Rank them using:

• Highest Search Volume
• Lowest Competition
• Highest Opportunity Score
• Current Trend
• User Search Intent

Display them as modern clickable cards.

Do NOT use tables.

Each card shows only:

• Keyword
• Search Volume
• Competition
• Trend

When one keyword is selected:

✓ Highlight it.

✓ Display "Selected".

✓ Lock the selected keyword.

✓ Automatically continue to the next step.

==================================================
STEP 3 — TITLE SUGGESTIONS
==================================================

Based ONLY on the selected keyword,

Generate the 5 strongest SEO article titles.

Titles must be:

• Human
• Attractive
• Click-worthy
• Helpful
• SEO Optimized

Rank using:

• Search Intent
• CTR Potential
• Ranking Opportunity

Display as clickable cards.

When one title is selected:

✓ Fill the Article Title automatically.

✓ Highlight the selected card.

✓ Generate the SEO Slug automatically.

==================================================
STEP 4 — SEO DATA
==================================================

Automatically generate:

• Focus Keyword

• Secondary Keywords

• Long-tail Keywords

• Semantic Keywords

• Entity Keywords

• User Intent

• Search Intent

• Target Audience

• Content Angle

Everything must be generated automatically.

No manual work.

• Examples

==================================================
STEP 6 — ARTICLE GENERATION
==================================================

After all previous steps,

Enable:

Generate Article

The administrator clicks only one button.

Everything else is automatic.

==================================================
ARTICLE QUALITY
==================================================

The article must be written like a real expert.

It must NEVER feel AI generated.

Human writing style.

Natural tone.

Easy to read.

Useful.

Helpful.

Trustworthy.

Unique.

No duplicate content.

No keyword stuffing.

No repetitive paragraphs.

No AI patterns.

The article must solve the visitor's problem before trying to rank.

Always prioritize user satisfaction.

==================================================

==================================================

Every article must include:

• Powerful Hook

• Real explanations

• Practical examples

• Step-by-Step Guide

• Frequently Asked Questions

• Common Mistakes

• Tips

• Best Practices

• Real comparison tables ONLY when useful

• Summary

• Conclusion

• Natural Call-To-Action

==================================================
RELATED CALCULATORS
==================================================

Suggest the best related calculator.

Insert a natural internal link inside the article.

Display a dedicated section:

Related Calculator

Suggest creating one inside the AI Calculator Factory.
