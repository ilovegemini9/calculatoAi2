==================================================
RESEARCH TOPIC WORKFLOW
==================================================

The "Research Topic" field must become an intelligent starting point for the entire article generation workflow.

As the administrator types a topic, the system must automatically retrieve live search data from the configured providers (Google Search, Google Autocomplete, Google Trends, People Also Ask, Reddit, Quora and other configured sources when available).

Do NOT require the administrator to enter the exact title manually.

Instead, automatically generate the 5 strongest topic suggestions.

Rank suggestions using:

• Highest Search Volume
• Lowest Competition
• Highest Opportunity Score
• Current Trend
• User Search Intent
• Content Potential

Display the suggestions as clean clickable cards below the Research Topic field.

Do NOT use tables.

Each suggestion card displays only:

• Topic
• Search Volume
• Competition (Low / Medium / High)
• Trend (↑ ↓)

When the administrator selects one suggestion:

✓ The Research Topic field is filled automatically.
✓ The selected card is highlighted.
✓ A "Selected" badge is displayed.
✓ The remaining workflow starts automatically.

==================================================
AUTOMATIC SEO WORKFLOW
==================================================

After selecting the Research Topic, the system automatically performs the following steps in order:

1. Generate the 5 best SEO Article Titles.

2. After selecting a Title:
   • Generate the SEO-friendly URL Slug automatically.

3. Generate the 5 best Focus Keywords related only to the selected title.

4. After selecting the Focus Keyword:
   • Generate Secondary Keywords.
   • Generate Long-tail Keywords.
   • Generate Semantic Keywords.
   • Generate Entity Keywords.

5. Generate the complete SEO outline automatically:
   • H1
   • H2
   • H3
   • Table of Contents
   • FAQ
   • How-To
   • Pros & Cons
   • Common Mistakes
   • Tips
   • Comparison Sections (only when useful)

6. Enable the "Generate Article" button only after all required selections are completed.

==================================================
ARTICLE GENERATION
==================================================

The generated article must prioritize helping the visitor before ranking.

It must never feel AI-generated.

Write like a real industry expert using a natural human tone.

The content must be unique, trustworthy, practical and easy to understand.

The article should include:

• Strong SEO Hook at the beginning.
• Clear introduction.
• Table of Contents.
• Well-structured H2/H3 hierarchy.
• Step-by-step explanations.
• Practical examples.
• Real-world use cases.
• Tips and best practices.
• Common mistakes to avoid.
• FAQ based on real user questions.
• Comparison tables only when they improve understanding.
• Bullet lists where appropriate.
• Helpful conclusion.
• Strong but natural Call-To-Action.

If the topic is related to one or more calculators available on the website:

• Automatically detect them.
• Suggest the most relevant calculator(s).
• Insert contextual internal links naturally inside the article.
• Display a "Related Calculators" section near the end.

If relevant blog articles already exist:

• Suggest related articles automatically.
• Create natural internal links.

Automatically generate:

• SEO Meta Title
• Meta Description
• Canonical URL
• OpenGraph
• Twitter Cards
• JSON-LD Schema
• Breadcrumb Schema
• FAQ Schema
• Article Schema
• Reading Time
• Last Updated

The article must be optimized for:

• Google Search
• Google AI Overview
• ChatGPT
• Gemini
• Claude
• Perplexity
• Voice Search
• Featured Snippets
• Entity SEO
• E-E-A-T
• Helpful Content
• Programmatic SEO compatibility

The final objective is not only to rank well in search engines, but to genuinely solve the visitor's problem and maximize user satisfaction.
